Iphone 16

The iPhone 16, released in September 2024, introduces several enhancements over its predecessor, the iPhone 15

Features

  • Processor: Powered by the Apple A18 chip
  • Connectivity: All models in the iPhone 16 lineup support Wi-Fi 7
  • Camera: Equipped with a 48-megapixel main camera

Pricing

The pricing for the iPhone 16 lineup is as follows: .

  • iPhone 16 Pro: Starts at $999 (approximately ₹82,000)
  • iPhone 16 Plus: Starts at $899 (approximately ₹74,000)
